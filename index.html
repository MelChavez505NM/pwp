About Melissa Chavez

As a seasoned tax and accounting professional, I bring a wealth of expertise in tax, auditing, and oil and gas accounting. My passion lies in leveraging industry knowledge to drive strategic financial decisions. Here’s a snapshot of my professional journey:


Forensic Tax Auditor

For two years, I spearheaded comprehensive tax account audits for New Mexico Tax and Revenue Department, uncovering critical tax findings to pursue complex tax fraud cases. With my mastered application of tax law, and the collaboration with Special Agents, the agency was able to close several high-profile tax investigation which resulted in tax collections of over 6 million for the New Mexico State Coffers.


Oil/Gas Royalty Auditor

As a seasoned manager in the oil and gas sector, I have led teams in maximizing royalty remittances to the State by spearheading groundbreaking technical research and analysis. My leadership ensured the accuracy of royalty returns, preventing financial discrepancies and safeguarding State revenues. I provided strategic direction to revenue accountants, resolving complex royalty issues and aligning operational practices with State statutes and industry standards. By collaborating with IT specialists, I guided the development of internal data reports, enhancing accuracy and streamlining decision-making processes across the organization. Under my leadership, the New Mexico Land Office achieved record-breaking revenue collections, significantly contributing to successful audits and the office’s billion-dollar revenue stream. I am also committed to fostering industry compliance, having developed and delivered comprehensive training programs that empower oil and gas remitters to accurately report royalties.
