About Melissa Chavez

As a seasoned tax and accounting professional, I bring a wealth of expertise in tax, auditing, and oil and gas accounting. My passion lies in leveraging deep industry knowledge to drive strategic financial decisions. Here’s a snapshot of my professional journey:


Forensic Tax Auditor

For two years, I spearheaded comprehensive tax account audits for New Mexico Tax and Revenue Department, uncovering critical evidence to pursue complex tax fraud cases. Mastered the application of tax law, ensuring accurate determination of tax liabilities and enforcement of compliance of Business organizations. Collaborated with Special Agents, providing technical tax expertise in high-profile investigations.


Oil/Gas Royalty Management Auditor

For over 12 years, in the intricate world of oil and gas, I conducted groundbreaking audits, maximizing royalty remittances by identifying key findings of oil and gas entities. Ensured accurate royalty returns, safeguarding State revenues. Developed internal data reports, streamlining decision-making processes and contributing to the Billion dollar New Mexico State Land Office's record-breaking revenue collections. Expertly audited oil and gas corporations, ensuring fiscal compliance and integrity. Assisted remitters in accurate reporting, enhancing compliance and reporting accuracy. Recovered substantial revenues through diligent assessment and demand notices.
